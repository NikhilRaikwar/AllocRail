AllocRail

Turn Dodo revenue into instant Solana treasury payouts.

User
SaaS and AI founders
Built for Indian and global founders paying distributed contractors and teams.
Wedge
Post-revenue treasury routing
Not another checkout product. Dodo handles collection; AllocRail handles treasury routing after revenue lands.
Problem

Global revenue is easier than global treasury.

  • Founders still split revenue in spreadsheets after payment lands.
  • Contractor payouts, tax reserves, and founder distributions are disconnected from billing truth.
  • Refunds and disputes create payout risk if routes execute too early.
  • There is no clean proof chain from customer payment to downstream treasury movement.
Solution

AllocRail is the programmable treasury layer after Dodo revenue lands.

Dodo checkout
-> verified webhook
-> allocation rule match
-> payout intents
-> founder approval / hold logic
-> wallet-signed Solana USDC settlement
-> audit receipt
Why this fits the track

Meaningful Dodo integration plus real-world stablecoin utility.

  • Dodo checkout sessions include routing metadata.
  • Signed Dodo webhooks are verified and idempotent.
  • Subscriptions, credits, refunds, and disputes all feed treasury logic.
  • Solana USDC is used for real multi-recipient treasury settlement, not just a token badge.
Proof

What is already built.

  • Founder dashboard with seeded demo treasury state
  • Revenue event inbox and grouped payout routes
  • Wallet binding and wallet-signed execution
  • Refund / dispute hold logic
  • Receipt history linking Dodo revenue to Solana proof
  • AI treasury copilot for rule drafting and queue summaries
