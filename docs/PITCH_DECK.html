Slide 2 · Problem

Global revenue is easier than global treasury.

  • Contractor splits still live in spreadsheets.
  • Tax reserves are tracked outside the billing system.
  • Refunds and disputes create payout risk.
  • Cross-border treasury workflows stay manual after payment succeeds.
Speaker note: The painful part starts after checkout, not before it.
Slide 3 · Solution

AllocRail is the programmable treasury layer after Dodo revenue lands.

Dodo checkout
-> verified webhook
-> allocation rule match
-> payout intents
-> founder approval / hold logic
-> wallet-signed Solana settlement
-> audit receipt
Speaker note: This is treasury routing and control, not another checkout wrapper.
